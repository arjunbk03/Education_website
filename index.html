Education in Nepal

Despite improvements in recent years, Nepal's education system still faces significant challenges, particularly in rural areas where access to quality education remains limited. However, there are organizations working tirelessly to improve the situation. The goal of this website is to provide a platform that connects individuals who are passionate about improving education in Nepal with these organizations.
